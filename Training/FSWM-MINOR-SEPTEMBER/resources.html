Student Resources

Be Smart. Use All Your Resources.

Like any worthy adventure, college can be full of challenges. Whatever yours may be, we’ve got the resources to guide you through. Whether you need help researching a topic, keeping up with your studies, registering or paying for classes, or adjusting to life in a new city or country... we’re here for you. Just ask

Library

Search our database of academic and research resources online or at one of our four campus libraries. Our collections include more than 100,000 books, 200 databases, 13,000 ebooks and videos, and so much more. Make an appointment with one of our librarians—they’re ready to help you navigate library resources and find the materials you need.

Tutoring

Sometimes you just need some help. We offer a variety of tutoring and academic support services, including peer and faculty-led tutoring in one-to-one and group settings, both onsite and online. Just find the area in which you prefer assistance, and schedule an appointment today!

Supplemental Instruction

Taking a course that’s known to be difficult? Joining your classmates for regularly scheduled Supplemental Instruction (SI) sessions can make it a lot easier. SI sessions are informal, peer-led discussion-based meetings that will help you review and reinforce what you’re learning in a particularly challenging class.

International Student Support

No matter how far you’ve come to attend New York Tech, we’ll do everything we can to make you feel at home. We have many services and programs, as well as organizations and clubs, focused specially on the needs of international students. From the day you arrive through your life as an alumnus, there will be someone here to guide you—whether you need help with travel plans and documentation, want to join a social event or club, or learn the ways of your new environment. Let us help!

Counseling & Wellness

To do your best at college you need to take good care of yourself. Eat well, get enough sleep, do something you enjoy every day. And if you notice that you’re feeling over-stressed or depressed or uncomfortably homesick, please don’t sit there and let it get worse—come to our campus Counseling Services in New York City or Long Island. Our licensed professional counselors have short-term strategies to help you bounce back and thrive for the long run.

Career Services

We’re here to help you make the most of your New York Tech degree. Enhance your education through internships, volunteer work, study abroad, and memberships in professional organizations and clubs. Our many resources can help you find the job you want, expand your professional network, and advance your career. Also be sure to check our the Career Corner blog for our latest news and events!

Study Abroad

There is great benefit to spending part of your college career going to school abroad. New York Tech’s Global Exchange Program makes it easy to enrich and continue your program of study by spending a year at one of our worldwide campuses—in Nanjing, Abu Dhabi, or Vancouver—without missing a beat. Or maybe you want to spend a semester in Denmark, the Netherlands, or China; winter break in India, the U.K., or Costa Rica; summer in Germany, Brazil, or France. When you’re ready to wander, we’ll help you get started.
